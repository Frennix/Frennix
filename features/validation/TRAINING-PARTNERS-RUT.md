# Training Partners — Beta tester runbook

**Time:** ~15 minutes · **Requires:** Complete profile + discovery enabled

## Steps

1. Open **Settings → Training partner preferences** — confirm city, goals, activities, gender filters.
2. Turn on **Discoverable as a training partner**.
3. Open **Settings → Find training partners** (or `/matching`).
4. **Skip** one athlete — confirm next card appears.
5. **Connect** with an athlete who also connects back (use a paired tester account).
6. Confirm **New Training Match** notification (in-app; push if backgrounded).
7. Open **Settings → Training matches** — see the match row with presence.
8. Tap **Message** — send a message; confirm delivery on other device.
9. Optional: **Remove** match — confirm row disappears; chat remains in Messages.

## Report issues

Use **Report an issue** at the bottom of the discovery screen, or **Settings → Send feedback** (area: Training Partners).

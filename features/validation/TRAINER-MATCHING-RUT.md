# Trainer Matching — Beta tester runbook

**Time:** ~20 minutes · **Athlete + trainer accounts**

## Athlete steps

1. **Discover → Find a trainer** (or Settings → Find a trainer).
2. Apply a filter (category, specialty, or city) — confirm results update.
3. Open a trainer profile — review bio, categories, certifications, portfolio.
4. Tap **Request to connect** with an optional intro message.
5. After trainer accepts, open **Trainer connections** and **Message**.

## Trainer steps

1. **Settings → Become a trainer** (first time) or **Trainer profile**.
2. Fill bio, categories, specialties, availability, coaching format.
3. Enable **Discoverable as a trainer**.
4. Upload at least one certification or portfolio photo (optional but recommended).
5. Open **Trainer connections** — accept a pending request.
6. Message the athlete.

## Report issues

Use **Report an issue** on discovery or connections screens, or **Settings → Send feedback** (area: Trainer Matching).
